**Objective:** Natural transition to premium products

**The Business Owner Conversation:**
*"[Name], you're clearly sophisticated about using tax strategies to keep more of what you earn. Let me ask you - are you using the same strategic thinking for your personal wealth accumulation? Most business owners aren't..."*

- **Infinite Banking:** "Your own private bank for business opportunities"

1. How much are you setting aside annually for retirement?
2. What's your biggest financial concern for the next 10 years?  
3. How important is leaving a legacy for your family?
4. Are you concerned about future tax rate increases?
5. What would happen to your business if you couldn't work?
